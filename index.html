The City Farm

A local charity providing training opportunities for vulnerable adults.

About Us

The City Farm is a small community farm that provides training opportunities for vulnerable adults, allowing them to develop skills and social confidence. We are entirely self-funded and rely on visitors, sales at our café, donations and memberships to sustain our work.

Visit Us

We welcome visitors to our farm and have a small café serving delicious homemade food made with fresh produce from the farm. You can also purchase our vegetables and other farm products at our farm shop.
